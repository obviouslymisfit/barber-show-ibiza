About

The story behind the craft

Brand Story

Ibiza-rooted, internationally fluent

Barber Show Ibiza was founded on a single conviction: that a barbershop should be a place where skill is visible and nothing is performed. Not the idea of craft — craft itself. The kind that takes years to develop, cannot be faked, and does not need to announce itself.

We opened in the centre of Eivissa because this island demands a particular kind of quality — its clientele is global, style-conscious, and experienced. They have sat in chairs across continents. They know when something is good and they know when it is not. We built the shop for them.

Alongside the barbershop, the studio emerged from the same thinking: that creative professionals in Ibiza deserve a space that matches the quality of their work. A location with real character — not a generic white box — where the environment contributes to the image rather than competing with it.

"Style is rarely accidental. It is built from small decisions — the angle of a fade, the balance of volume, the way texture moves with natural growth."

The People

The hands behind the work

Three barbers with distinct approaches and a shared standard. Getting to know who is behind the chair is part of choosing the right one.

Senior Barber · Founder

Marc

Fourteen years behind the chair across Barcelona, London, and Ibiza. Marc built the shop around the values he found missing elsewhere — patience, precision, and no unnecessary conversation when you would rather just sit in the chair.

Master Barber

Alejandro

Alejandro trained in Madrid and refined his technique in Amsterdam before arriving in Ibiza. His specialty is skin fades and beard sculpting — he approaches both with the same methodical attention. A consultation with Alejandro feels like a conversation about shape.

Barber & Style Consultant

Carlos

Carlos brings a background in fashion alongside his barbering craft — the two inform each other. He reads texture, proportion, and lifestyle when he looks at a client. If you want something new and want to understand why it works, Carlos is the one to talk to.

Location

In the heart of Eivissa

We are located at Carrer de Josep Riquer Llobet, 7, Local 5, in the centre of Eivissa town. Close to the old city, easy to reach on foot or by transport, and open to both residents and visitors.

Ibiza's creative community is international and discerning. The island draws people who know quality from experience — and expect it without having to ask. Being here is a statement about what we believe the place deserves.

Beyond the Chair

The studio: a second offering

Creative professionals in Ibiza often find the same thing missing — a space that has real character, proper equipment, and none of the compromise. The Barber Show studio was built to fill exactly that space.

Photographers

Controllable light, seamless backdrops, and a distinctive environment that adds depth to portraits and editorial work.

Videographers

A space that works for both interview setups and creative shoots. Textured walls, timber, and the option to dress the space as needed.

Brands & Agencies

Campaign shoots, product photography, content days — the studio holds a full day's work and is available Monday through Saturday.
