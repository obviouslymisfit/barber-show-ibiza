Our Story

A shop built on deliberate choices

Barber Show Ibiza was not built for the crowd. It was built for people who know what they want and value the skill behind it.

We exist at the intersection of grooming and creative culture. The barbershop is precise and unhurried. The studio alongside it is built for professionals who need a space that matches the quality of their work. Both share the same conviction: that intentional work, done with restraint, produces results that last.

Creative Studio

A space built for serious work

The studio at Barber Show is more than an empty room. It is a considered creative environment — equipped, distinctive, and ready.

Photographers, filmmakers, content creators and brands rent it by the hour, half-day, or full day. Natural light, backdrops, professional equipment and character that most studios cannot manufacture.
