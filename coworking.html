The Space

Character that most studios cannot manufacture

The Barber Show studio exists within the same building as the barbershop — which means it carries the same atmosphere. Warm, deliberate, and built from real materials.

This is not a white box rented by the hour to anyone who books. It is a specific place, with specific light, in a specific part of Ibiza. That specificity is what makes it useful. The environment enters your images and your footage — it does not disappear.

The studio is suitable for portrait photography, editorial shoots, commercial campaigns, video interviews, behind-the-scenes content, and any creative work that benefits from a distinctive location with professional infrastructure already in place.

Every Booking Includes

What's included

No surprises. Every studio booking — regardless of duration — comes with the following. Nothing billed separately.

Natural & Artificial Light

Controllable window light from the north-facing wall, supplemented by professional continuous LED lighting. Adjust to the shoot.

Backdrop System

Full-width motorised backdrop rail with three standard colours — warm white, mid grey, and charcoal. Additional rolls available on request.

Styling Chair & Barber Set

The original barber chair and mirror wall can be incorporated as a distinctive shooting environment. Subject to scheduling with the barbershop.

Changing Area & Storage

Private changing area for talent, with hanging rail and surface space for wardrobe. Secure storage for equipment during the booking.

Wi-Fi & Power

High-speed broadband and multiple power access points throughout the studio. Suitable for tethered shooting and real-time file transfer.

Refreshments

Coffee, water, and basic refreshments for the duration of the booking. No catering, but the space is within walking distance of several good options.
